Paste the note shell, formatting structure, or procedural defaults for this profile, then mark what must stay exact versus what SurgiNote should recognize and apply as your usual style.

Green = keep format / must-copy language exactly
Purple = recognize this preference and apply it when appropriate
Good purple examples: usual tools, suture choice, routine operative steps, favorite structure.
How SurgiNote Uses This
EXACT Preserves format, headings, and any language you marked to keep exactly.
PREFERENCES Recognizes your usual operative steps, tools, sutures, and procedural quirks without forcing verbatim copy.
AUTO APPLY Matches the profile against note type and case context, then pulls it in automatically when it fits.
